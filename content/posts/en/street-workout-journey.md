Two years ago I couldn’t do a single strict pull-up. Today I want to share how bar training changed me.

## Start with the Basics

Many people chase muscle-ups and front levers on day one and end up injured or quitting. My rule: **build the foundation first**.

- Pull-ups: the base for pulling strength;
- Dips: pushing power;
- Core control: prerequisite for every static hold.

> There are no shortcuts — only daily accumulation.

## What Training Changed

The bar reshaped more than my body:

1. **Focus** — on the bar you can’t multitask; the movement demands full attention;
2. **Patience** — one skill can take months to unlock;
3. **Discipline** — your body gives honest feedback; skipping work shows up fast.

## Closing

If you want to start, remember: **don’t compare yourself to others — only to yesterday’s you.** Begin with one pull-up and stay consistent. You’ll be surprised what your body can do.

See you at the bar.
